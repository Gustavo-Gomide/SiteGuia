Scheme — Recursos Avancados

Controle de fluxo de alto nivel em Scheme

Continuations, TCO, IO por ports e valores multiplos tornam Scheme extremamente expressivo.

Esses recursos parecem abstratos no inicio, mas resolvem problemas reais de controle, performance e composicao.

Continuations com call/cc

Scheme — call-with-current-continuation
(define (buscar-primeiro-negativo xs)
	(call/cc
	 (lambda (escape)
		 (for-each
			(lambda (x)
				(when (< x 0)
					(escape x)))
			xs)
		 #f)))

(buscar-primeiro-negativo '(4 8 -3 9)) ; -3

; backtracking simplificado e "exceptions" com continuation:
(define (safe-div a b)
	(call/cc
	 (lambda (throw)
		 (if (= b 0)
				 (throw 'erro-divisao-por-zero)
				 (/ a b)))))

Tail calls e trampolining

Scheme — TCO e trampoline pattern
; R7RS exige proper tail calls
(define (soma-tail n acc)
	(if (= n 0)
			acc
			(soma-tail (- n 1) (+ acc n))))

; trampoline basico
(define (trampoline thunk)
	(let loop ((t thunk))
		(if (procedure? t)
				(loop (t))
				t)))

(define (f n acc)
	(if (= n 0)
			acc
			(lambda () (f (- n 1) (+ acc n)))))

(trampoline (lambda () (f 100000 0)))

Ports e I/O

Scheme — open-input-file, with-input-from-file, read e write
(define p (open-input-file "dados.scm"))
(define x (read p))
(close-input-port p)

(with-input-from-file "dados.scm"
	(lambda ()
		(let ((v (read)))
			(display v)
			(newline))))

(define out (open-output-string))
(write '(1 2 3) out)
(define s (get-output-string out))
(display s)

Valores multiplos

Scheme — values, call-with-values e define-values
(define (div-mod a b)
	(values (quotient a b) (remainder a b)))

(call-with-values
 (lambda () (div-mod 17 5))
 (lambda (q r)
	 (display q)
	 (display " ")
	 (display r)
	 (newline)))

(define-values (q r) (div-mod 23 7))
(list q r)

Valores multiplos evitam criar pares/tuplas temporarias em APIs que naturalmente retornam mais de um resultado.
