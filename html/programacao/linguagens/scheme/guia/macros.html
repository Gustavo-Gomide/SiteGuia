Scheme — Macros com syntax-rules

Codigo que gera codigo com higiene automatica

Macros em Scheme expandem sintaxe em tempo de compilacao, mantendo seguranca de escopo por padrao.

Macros higienicas reduzem bugs de captura de variavel, um problema comum em sistemas de macro nao higienicos.

define-syntax e syntax-rules

Scheme — padroes, templates e ellipsis (...)
(define-syntax my-when
	(syntax-rules ()
		((_ cond body ...)
		 (if cond
				 (begin body ...)
				 #f))))

(my-when (> 5 3)
	(display "ok")
	(newline))

Exemplos praticos de macros

Scheme — my-and, my-or, while e swap!
(define-syntax my-and
	(syntax-rules ()
		((_ ) #t)
		((_ x) x)
		((_ x y ...) (if x (my-and y ...) #f))))

(define-syntax my-or
	(syntax-rules ()
		((_ ) #f)
		((_ x) x)
		((_ x y ...) (let ((t x)) (if t t (my-or y ...))))))

(define-syntax while
	(syntax-rules ()
		((_ cond body ...)
		 (let loop ()
			 (when cond
				 body ...
				 (loop))))))

(define-syntax swap!
	(syntax-rules ()
		((_ a b)
		 (let ((tmp a))
			 (set! a b)
			 (set! b tmp)))))

syntax-case (R6RS/Racket)

syntax-case habilita macros procedurais, com mais controle sobre validacoes e transformacoes sintaticas.

Racket — macro procedural com syntax-case
#lang racket
(define-syntax (my-inc stx)
	(syntax-case stx ()
		[(_ x)
		 #'(set! x (+ x 1))]))

(define n 10)
(my-inc n)
n

Ferramentas como gensym e bibliotecas SRFI (por exemplo, SRFI-92) ampliam ainda mais composicao de macros.
