Base solida para programar em Scheme

Os fundamentos de Scheme giram em torno de expressoes, funcoes, listas e escopo lexico.

Quando voce domina pares e recursao em Scheme, domina boa parte da essencia de linguagens funcionais.

Funcoes e recursao

Scheme — define, recursao e accumulator pattern
(define (fatorial n)
	(if (<= n 1)
			1
			(* n (fatorial (- n 1)))))

(define (fatorial-tail n)
	(define (loop k acc)
		(if (<= k 1)
				acc
				(loop (- k 1) (* acc k))))
	(loop n 1))

Listas e operacoes

Scheme — map, filter, for-each, apply, length, append
(define xs '(1 2 3 4 5))

(map (lambda (x) (* x x)) xs)
(filter odd? xs)
(for-each display xs)
(apply + xs)
(length xs)
(append xs '(6 7 8))

Escopo com let, let* e letrec

Scheme — escopo lexico, closures e currying manual
(let ((x 2) (y 3))
	(+ x y))

(let* ((x 2)
			 (y (+ x 10)))
	(* x y))

(letrec ((par? (lambda (n)
								 (if (= n 0) #t (impar? (- n 1)))))
				 (impar? (lambda (n)
									 (if (= n 0) #f (par? (- n 1))))))
	(par? 10))

(define (soma-com a)
	(lambda (b) (+ a b)))

(define soma10 (soma-com 10))
(soma10 5) ; 15
